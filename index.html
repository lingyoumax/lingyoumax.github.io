    自动微分及其在系统建模中的应用
    Automatic differentiation and its application in system modeling

    介绍

    自从导数的概念被提出以后，导数已经给人们的生产生活提供了很多的便利，比如人们可以用它预测某件商品在市场中价格的走势。然而在过去，人们为了得到它的值都是通过纸和笔去计算，这不仅花费大量的时间，还容易因为人们的疏忽而得到错误的结果。并且在生产中，人们发现有些重要的方程是很难被人求解的。随着计算机的出现以及普及，人们意识到它能够帮助我们计算导数.正是在这种情况下，有人提出了自动微分这一概念。
    Since the concept of derivative was introduced, it has provided a lot of convenience to people’s production life, such as people can use it to predict the trend of the price of a certain commodity in the market. However, in the past, people used to calculate its value by paper and pencil, which not only took a lot of time, but also gave wrong results due to people’s negligence. And in production, it was found that some important equations were difficult to be solved by people. With the advent of computers and their popularity, people realized that it could help us to calculate derivatives.It is in this case that the concept of automatic differentiation was introduced.
