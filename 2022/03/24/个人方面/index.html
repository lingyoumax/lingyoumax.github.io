为什么去德国

Why go to Germany

  • 我认为德国拥有良好的教育资源，我渴望得到机会去那以获得我的硕士学位。
  • I think Germany has good educational resources and I am eager to get the opportunity to go there to get my master’s degree.

    你想申请哪个学校？优势和专业

    Which school do you want to apply for? What is its advantages.Which specialty do you want to apply for?
  • 我想申请的是慕尼黑工业大学的计算机科学与技术专业，这是我本科所学习的专业，我想继续在这方面深造。这所大学注重创新，我非常欣赏这一点。
  • I want to apply for the major of computer science and technology in Technical University of Munich, which is my undergraduate major. I want to continue my further study in this field.This university focuses on the quality of innovation, which I appreciate very much

    语言学多久了？什么时候考试？

    How long have you been studying language? When will we take the exam?
  • 我申请所需要的语言是英语，为此我已经努力学些了半年。我将会在下个月参加考试。
  • The language I need in my application is English.For this reason, I have studied hard for half a year.I will take the exam next month.

    你是否了解留学一年的生活费?

    Do you know the cost of studying abroad for one year?
  • 据我所知，德国留学的生活费是861欧元每月，一年就是10332欧元。但我的父母为了以防万一，多准备了10000欧元。
  • As far as I know, the living cost of studying in Germany is 861 euros per month, 10332 euros a year. But my parents prepared an extra 10000 euros just in case.

    你在德国是否有亲人

    Do you have any relatives in Germany
  • 没有，我的所有亲人都在中国。
  • No, all my relatives are in China.

    你毕业之后想干什么

    What do you want to do after graduation
  • 我想要回国工作，想在我父母老去的时候照顾他们。
  • I want to go back to China and have a job,so that I can take care of my parents when they get older.

    描述一下你所学习的专业

    Describe your major
  • 我的专业主要讲述了我们如何运用计算机来帮助我们，以及它能给我们带来什么好处，比如说我可以设计一个程序去帮助那些有需要的人。
  • My major mainly talks about how we use computers to help us and what benefits it can bring us. For example, I can design a program to help those in need.
